MUSC, USC Greenville, and LSU NOLA were all investigated — none work for a Canadian on OPT. Documented here so no one loses a week revisiting them.

MUSC College of Medicine

CUT

Charleston, SC · MD

Dealbreaker: MUSC requires applicants to be US citizens or lawful permanent residents (green card). OPT status does not qualify.

Source: MUSC College of Medicine admissions eligibility page. Confirmed April 2026.

USC School of Medicine Greenville

CUT

Greenville, SC · MD

Dealbreaker: USC Greenville is not SEVP-certified to issue I-20s to international students — they cannot legally admit a Canadian citizen. USC School of Medicine Columbia has the same policy.

Source: USC SOM Greenville international applicant FAQ + SEVP school lookup. Confirmed April 2026.

LSU Health New Orleans SOM

CUT

New Orleans, LA · MD

Dealbreaker: LSU Admissions Criteria states “Applications are not accepted from International Students.” SDN-confirmed: Canadian applicants are instructed to bring a green card to the interview. OPT does not qualify. Tulane (already on reapply list) is the LA substitute.

Source: LSU NOLA Admissions Criteria + SDN 2025-2026 thread. Verified by Wanda Run 5, 2026-04-16. Full deep-dive archive on Wanda's page.

📋 AACOMAS Application Guide

What is AACOMAS?

The centralized application for DO schools, like AMCAS is for MD schools. You CAN and SHOULD apply to both simultaneously. Thousands of applicants do this every year.

Key Differences from AMCAS

  • • Different GPA calculation method
  • • Personal statement should reflect osteopathic philosophy
  • • Separate transcript submission process
  • • $198 first school + $60 per additional
